================================================================================
PART 2 — SALES ORDER CREDIT VALIDATION
================================================================================

Extend:
sale.order

Business Rule:

When confirming a sales order:

Retrieve:
- Customer credit limit
- Current receivable balance
- Total uninvoiced confirmed sales orders
- Current sales order amount

Compute:

Exposure = Receivable Balance
         + Uninvoiced Orders
         + Current Order

Validation Logic:

If:
Exposure > Credit Limit

Then:
1. Block confirmation
2. Raise ValidationError
3. Create a log entry in sd.credit.exposure.log
4. Status = blocked

Otherwise:
1. Allow confirmation
2. Create successful log entry
3. Continue standard Odoo workflow
